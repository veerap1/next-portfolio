About

Focused on practical DevOps growth

I care about making cloud and platform work understandable: what was built, how it was deployed, and what was learned from it.

This portfolio combines project work with written thinking. Instead of only listing tools, it shows how I learn: by building labs, documenting workflows, debugging failures, and turning notes into repeatable knowledge.

The goal is simple: make my progress visible in a way that feels credible to recruiters, useful to other learners, and easy for me to keep updating over time.
